Privacy Policy

Last updated: 4 June 2026

1. Who we are

The SC// Infrastructure Sizer ("the Sizer", "we", "us") is operated by Demolabs. For any privacy question, or to exercise your rights, contact us at privacy@demolabs.me.

2. What data we collect

  • Account data — your email address and a securely hashed password (we never store your password in plain text). Your email domain determines your organisation/tenant.
  • Saved sizings — the configurations you choose to save, including any workload data you import (e.g. Live Optics / RVTools exports) and the options you select.
  • Usage & security records — sign-in timestamps, an audit log of account actions (sign-up, activation, password changes, administrative actions), and limited technical data needed to operate the service.

3. Why we use it, and our lawful basis

  • To provide the service (authenticate you, store and share your sizings within your organisation) — performance of a contract.
  • To keep the service secure (audit logging, brute-force protection) — legitimate interests.
  • To verify your email and send password-reset messages, where enabled — performance of a contract / legitimate interests.
  • To send product update emails — for example, a notification when new hardware models become available — where you have opted in. This is based on your consent, and you can opt out at any time.

4. Cookies

We use a single, strictly-necessary session cookie for the sole purpose of keeping you signed in. It is first-party, marked HttpOnly and SameSite=Lax, contains no advertising or tracking identifiers, and is removed when you sign out or it expires. Because it is essential to a service you explicitly request (logging in), it is exempt from prior-consent requirements under the ePrivacy rules, so no cookie-consent banner is shown. We do not use analytics, advertising, profiling, or any third-party cookies. If that ever changes, we will ask for your consent first.

5. Who we share it with

We do not sell your data. Saved sizings are visible to other users within your organisation (your email domain) and to our administrators for support. Beyond your own organisation, your data is shared only with Scale Computing, the vendor whose hardware this tool sizes, to provide and support the sizing service. We do not share your data with any other third parties.

6. How long we keep it (retention)

  • Account & sizings — kept while your account is active.
  • Deleted sizings — soft-deleted and recoverable by an administrator for 90 days, then permanently purged.
  • Disabled / inactive accounts — disabled accounts are purged after 90 days; accounts with no sign-in for a prolonged period (around a year) may be removed.
  • Audit log — retained for security and accountability. Any personal identifier of a deleted user is automatically anonymised exactly 365 days after that user is deleted; the (now non-personal) action record is kept.

7. Your rights

Subject to applicable law, you may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data, and may object to or restrict certain processing. You can delete your saved sizings yourself in the app; to delete your account or exercise any other right, contact privacy@demolabs.me. This tool is intended for use in an international setting, so the relevant data protection authority varies — you have the right to lodge a complaint with the supervisory authority in your own country or region.

8. Security

Passwords are hashed with a salted, slow key-derivation function (PBKDF2-SHA256). Access is restricted by role, sign-in is rate-limited, and the service is intended to be served over HTTPS. No system is perfectly secure, but we take appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your data.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated by email, and the "last updated" date above will change.
